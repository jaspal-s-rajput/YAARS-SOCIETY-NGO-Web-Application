Our Vision


The vision of YAARS SOCIETY is to transform society into a Thalassemia-free society. They aspire to eradicate instances where individuals succumb due to a shortage of blood, making it easily accessible to all in need. Their overarching goal is to ensure that no one loses their life due to a lack of basic medical resources, particularly blood, which is vital for the survival of Thalassemia patients. Through their dedication and community engagement, YAARS SOCIETY aims to create a healthier and more compassionate society, where every individual has access to essential medical care and support.

Envisions a future where society stands as a beacon of health and compassion, free from the burden of Thalassemia-related fatalities. Their mission is to eliminate the tragic loss of lives resulting from blood shortages by ensuring universal access to this life-saving resource. Their unwavering commitment extends beyond mere survival; they strive to nurture a community where everyone thrives, supported by comprehensive medical care and a network of solidarity. By fostering a culture of empathy and active participation, YAARS SOCIETY aims to cultivate a society where no individual is left behind due to preventable health challenges.

Our vision encompasses not only the eradication of Thalassemia but also the promotion of holistic well-being, paving the way for a brighter, healthier future for all.

History of YAARS - SOCIETY


In the bustling city of Indore, amidst the corridors of higher education, destiny wove together the paths of five compassionate souls â€“ Yash, Adnan, Akshay, Ritesh, and Shubham. It was the year 2011 when these young individuals, driven by a shared desire to make a tangible difference in their community, embarked on a journey that would shape the lives of countless others.
As college students immersed in their studies, they witnessed firsthand the struggles faced by those in need, particularly individuals battling the challenges of Thalassemia, a condition that demanded regular blood transfusions for survival. Fueled by empathy and a sense of duty towards their fellow citizens, they resolved to take action.

Pooling together their resources and bound by a common purpose, Yash, Adnan, Akshay, Ritesh, and Shubham initiated a grassroots movement aimed at addressing the pressing issue of blood scarcity. They began by organizing blood donation drives within their college campus, rallying their peers and faculty members to join their cause. Their efforts soon garnered attention beyond the confines of academia, drawing support from the wider community. With each successful blood donation camp, they witnessed the transformative power of collective action and realized the potential to effect broader change. However, their vision extended beyond immediate relief to encompass sustainable solutions and long-term impact. Recognizing the need for systemic intervention, they expanded their focus to advocate for greater awareness about Thalassemia and the importance of regular blood donations. As their grassroots movement gained momentum, Yash, Adnan, Akshay, Ritesh, and Shubham formalized their commitment by establishing YAARS SOCIETY in 2011. The name, derived from the initials of its founders, symbolized their camaraderie and shared mission.

Over the years, YAARS SOCIETY evolved from a small group of college friends to a dynamic force for social change. Their dedication to serving humanity remained unwavering, guiding them through challenges and triumphs alike. In 2022, YAARS SOCIETY achieved official NGO status, marking a significant milestone in their journey of service and solidarity. As they continue to expand their reach and impact, the legacy of Yash, Adnan, Akshay, Ritesh, and Shubham lives on, inspiring generations to come to strive for a more compassionate and equitable world.
